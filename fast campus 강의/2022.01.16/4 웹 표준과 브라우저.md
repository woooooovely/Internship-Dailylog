# 📌 W3C 표준화 제정 단계

1. **초안**(Working Draft, WD)
2. **후보권고안**(Candidate Recommendation, CR)
3. **제안권고안**(Proposed Recommendation, PR)
4. **권고안**(W3C Recommendation, REC)
